Searching for a good atmosphere? You've found it.


Here at the pub, we're all about beer and people.

The Shoulder of Mutton is a CAMRA award-winning corner pub, pleasantly perched in the centre of Wantage in Oxfordshire.


We sell craft beer, offer accommodation and host social events.

Our speciality is creating the ideal place for you to enjoy skillfully served real ales.
